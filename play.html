Assignment: Go Play With A Music App

Due: Tuesday, January 22nd in Class

Objectives:

  1. To help you understand the context of a music app on the iOS.
  2. To check and improve your presentation skills.

Download and play with an app that pertains to helping musicians or helping people learn music on an iPhone or iPad via iTunes. In a slide deck (e.g., PowerPoint) of no more than 5 slides, answer the following questions about the app that you downloaded:

  1. What is the purpose of the app?
  2. What are the core features of the app?
  3. What is appealing about the app?
  4. What are the limitations of the app?
  5. How would you improve the app?

IMPORTANT: The app cannot be GarageBand.

You may also demonstrate the app briefly but that is not critical.

Each italicized word in the questions above has significance in iOS development and ultimately, in this course.

Please be prepared to talk about your app, 4 minutes maximum, to the class on Tuesday.
