Assignment: The Soundboard

Due: Thursday, February 14th

Objectives

  • Write your first iOS app
  • Be exposed to the Model-View-Controller (MVC) design pattern
  • Use and understand basic user interface and user interaction: views, buttons, actions, and alerts

Instructions

In this first app, you will build a soundboard for the iPad. The idea is simple: pick a theme or character; press a button to play a sound related to the theme or character. Two Flash examples to illustrate the idea:

  • The Howard Stern Soundboard
  • Uncle Herbert (of Family Guy fame) Soundboard

Requirements

  • You must have a minimum of 5 buttons (and thus 5 sounds)
  • The sounds must be in MP3 format
  • You must have one button where upon clicking on it opens up the iTunes or YouTube app on your iPad!
  • You must have one "About" button where upon clicking on it opens up an alert that displays information about the app (e.g., version number, your name --the author, etc.)

Submitting Your Work

Push your work to a GitHub repository under your username and email me the Git URL (https) of the repo.
