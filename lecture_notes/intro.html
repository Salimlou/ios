Course Introduction

1. A look back at last year's class: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CM_3jaO_VXE

2. What this course is

  • A very special opportunity
  • Learn-by-doing
  • Real-world development
  • Teamwork
  • Learn from other students and teams in the class
  • A gift from Steinway, the world's greatest piano maker

3. The goal of this course in one sentence: build iPad apps for musicians or for people who want to learn music

4. What this course is NOT

  • Build clones of Spotify, last.fm
  • Build apps that uses the Echonest / last.fm / Spotify APIs
  • Build apps to manage your personal music library
  • Build DJ apps

5. Expectations

  • We are all in this together
  • No one is an expert on this stuff
  • We can't teach you everything; independent learning and curiosity are necessary.
  • We will teach you the basics

6. Outcomes (Paul)

7. Administrative

  • No textbook for this course
  • No exams in this course
  • No teaching assistant in this course. Paul and I will be providing more oversight and guidance to each team this semester.
  • No grade of an "Incomplete" will be given
  • Grades is based the entire development process of your team's app (more below in "Roadmap")
  • For students enrolled in the class, please complete the roster / survey form (it was emailed)
  • Requirements: (1) a Mac with Mac OS X Snow Leopard, Lion, or Mountain Lion and (2) Xcode (has iOS SDK) --free from Mac App Store
  • Not a requirement: an iPad. We have 10 iPad 2s to loan out.
  • All examples and lectures on GitHub: https://github.com/tuftsdev/ios
  • The course website available at: http://www.cs.tufts.edu/comp/150ISW/ which will redirect you to http://tuftsdev.github.com/ios/
  • Please keep up with course website for news and announcements
  • Notes and content of this course will also be available on iTunes U: https://itunes.apple.com/us/course/music-apps-for-the-ipad/id593471258

8. Granoff Lab Policy

  • Hours (building closes at 11 PM, lab at 12 AM, not open some holidays)
  • No food or drink except bottled water!
  • iPads are locked in a cabinet, we will give you the key code. Keep it locked.
  • You must sign out an iPad if it leaves the room. Simple clipboard / form.
  • Not open during classes: M-W 4:30 - 5:45 PM, Tu-Th 3 - 4:15 PM maybe, Sat 9 - 3 PM maybe
  • We will provide server space, but you should have your own backups!

9. Roadmap

  1. Generate app ideas
  2. Understand the iOS
  3. Learn Objective-C
  4. Practice using Xcode
  5. Determine teams (you choose) for project; project proposal
  6. Understand the many iOS SDK APIs and capabilities (ongoing)
  7. Design app
  8. Code, test, commit, push, and tune
  9. Present to Steinway in May

10. The first assignment: go play with a music app!
